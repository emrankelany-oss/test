Foodics was evolving from being known mainly as a POS provider to
wanting to be seen as the complete F&B growth platform — offering
payments, lending, data, marketplaces, and more.
-
But the market still largely perceived them as just a POS system.
-
They needed to educate thousands of restaurant owners and
operators about these new tools.
-
They also needed to establish clear market leadership, especially
as competition was increasing in Saudi and beyond.
PROBLEM
SOLUTION
✅ Created a flagship annual stage to position Foodics as the
authority shaping the future of F&B tech.
✅ Crafted end-to-end event narratives that showed how Foodics
solves merchant problems — from payments (Foodics Pay) to
financing (Foodics Capital) to scaling via Marketplace.
✅ Designed experiences that educated & excited: live demos, case
studies, data insights, and launches that made complex products
feel essential and easy.
✅ Transformed perception — so Foodics was no longer seen just
as a POS provider, but the growth engine for F&B businesses.
