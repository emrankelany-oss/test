Card anatomy — 4 elements, nothing more
  • 1 · The image — one flagship visual, full-bleed. Uses the real repo assets: public/assets/case-foodics-boundless.png & case-zid-ripple.png. Tinted to the brand color so the card reads even while moving.
  • 2 · Index tag — "CASE 01 / 02" mono pill. Signals there's a body of work, builds anticipation.
  • 3 · The proof metric — one number only: $2M→$1B, +200% YoY. This is the hook that makes you keep scrolling.
  • 4 · Name + caption — serif client name + tiny mono descriptor (campaign · sector). Footer adds disciplines + year, very low contrast.

How many? 2 in the Hero (Foodics + Zid) — the two deep case studies. They reappear as the Featured sections, so the Hero card literally is the door to that section (the Obsidian "persistent anchor" idea, applied to your work).

Motion: they fly in from the screen edges during State B, 3D-tilted (rotateY ~14–22°), each at a different parallax speed/depth, slightly blurred at the far depth. They are not clickable in the Hero — they're a promise; the click happens in Section 5/6.
