What it is: a sculpted, chunky 3D letter "M" — the Motion Agency mark — carved from a dark graphite/obsidian material. The object literally performs the line "we release momentum." It evolves v16's existing MMonument.tsx (already a 3D "M") + adds the asteroid-style energy cracks & rupture you liked in /frames. Brand + spectacle + reuses the pipeline.
